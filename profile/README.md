# Who We Are
KnowYi Technologies is an advanced manufacturing platform shortening the R&D-to-production cycle for North American hard-tech.

We provide a controlled, software-defined bridge between North American engineering teams and IATF 16949-certified supply chains in Asia. By automating the transition from messy BOMs and drawings to install-ready parts, we reduce traditional 12-week lead times to 2–3 weeks.

Our Capabilities:
• High-Precision Execution: CNC, PCBA, and robotics sub-assemblies with automotive-grade tolerances.
• Software-Driven QC: Proprietary backend for automated RFQ, revision control, and comprehensive QC logging (photos, material certs, and dimension checks).
• Institutional Trust: A single supplier of record in Canada, pre-qualified for federal/provincial procurement and top-tier research labs.

We handle the end-to-end complexity of cross-border execution (from engineering review to final delivery) so robotics, medical, and automotive teams can focus on innovation, not supply chain friction.
